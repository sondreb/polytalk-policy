PolyTalk Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2024-12-12

Information Collection

PolyTalk does not collect personal or account information directly. However, we use third-party services for analytics and advertising.

Analytics Data

We use Google Analytics to understand website usage patterns. Google Analytics may collect:

  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Browser and device information
  • Geographic location (country/region level)
  • Referral sources

Advertising Data

Our service uses Adcash for displaying advertisements. Adcash may collect:

  • Cookie data
  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Browser information

How Information is Used

Data collection occurs through the following services:

  • Google Analytics: To analyze website usage and improve user experience
  • Google Tag Manager: To manage and deploy tracking scripts
  • Adcash: To display advertisements and measure ad performance

Your Choices

You can control your data privacy through these methods:

  • Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Learn more about Adcash's privacy practices at Adcash Privacy Policy

Advertising

We use Adcash to display advertisements. Adcash uses cookies and similar technologies to serve ads and measure their performance. For more information about how Adcash handles your data, please visit their Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
